Getting to hotel

Once you arrive in Panamá, it’s time to check in! Our partnered hotels are a quick trip from the airport and to The Day After venue, Figali Plaza.

Taxis

Unlike most countries, taxis in Panamá do not have a taximeter. You discuss the fare price upfront before starting your journey. Some cab drivers have an overview of the city with price indications, depending on the district you are traveling to and from.

Shuttles

Many hotels offer a shuttle service from the airport, so be sure to check with yours before your arrival.

Uber

Panamá City is the fifth city in Latin America to join the Uber family. This cashless and convenient app allows you to request your own personal driver anytime, anywhere at the push of a button. The best part is that there is no unnecessary waiting – you can see where your car is on a live map and you’ll even get a notification when it’s arriving. After your ride is complete, payment is processed automatically through your credit card on file.

Getting to venue

The home of The Day After is the Figali Plaza Convention Center. Originally built in 2003 for the Miss Universe Pagent, Figali Plaza has a high-class structure and architecture, making it one of the best, if not the best, convention centers in Central America.

Taxis

Taxis are easy to flag down in Panamá City and will of course be readily available to you as you leave Figali Plaza. Since the venue is very close to most of the sites, expect to pay around $5 in fare to Figali Plaza from the city center and vice versa.
